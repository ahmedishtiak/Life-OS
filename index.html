Settings & Data

Your data lives in this browser
About this app

Life OS is a single HTML file. All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage on this exact URL. Nothing is sent anywhere. Nothing is shared. If you clear your browser data or open from a different device, your data won't be there — export regularly.

For a complete backup strategy: export the JSON monthly, commit it to a private repo or save to Proton Drive. The accompanying Excel workbook handles deep finance work.
